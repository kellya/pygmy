Shorty

Create a shortened URL with a random destination

To create a URL that will look something like /+x, simply add your destination to the URL field on the form and hit the submit button.

Upon successful submit, you will see the reseults page which will show your new shortened URL

URL format?

You may specify the URL with or without the scheme (the http:// part at the beginning of the URL). Note that this service only deals with WEB redirects, so you may not use anything other than http or https

If you do not specify a scheme, it will default to http://, which will typically just work (though you may get an addtional https redirect).

Create a custom keyword-based URL

Shorty also allows you to optionally add a /keyword URL type too. If you specify something in the keyword field, you will get a random short URL AND your custom string. Both will work when you specify the destiation name
